To The Rescue

“To The Rescue” is an aqua robotic event in which you will have to design and build a remote controlled rescue ship.

To The Rescue

Rescue ships know no limits they are put to sea when others seek the protection. There are several disasters that occur at sea. You will have to build a rescue ship which under adverse conditions will have to rescue people. Minimising the loss of life, injury, property damage or loss by rendering aid to persons in distress will be your priority for this event. This event will test your knowledge about the basics of ship and will challenge you to apply your skills for practical use. In the event your model of the ship will be evaluated for speed, stability and handling.
Dimensions of rescue ship: Length- 60cm , Width- 13.82cm and Draft- 3.65cm

Stages and Timeline

The event will be organised in two rounds. In each round you will be given a different situation and your rescue ship will have to successfully complete it in order to progress to the next round or to be considered for final evaluation.

Stage 1: Rescue

The main function of a rescue ship is to save people in case of any mishappening. Consider a situation where a merchant ship is sinking and you have been given the task to save the people. While the ship was sinking people got into 5 lifeboats and are now stranded at sea.

Stage 2: Firefighting

Fire is one of the calamities occurring at sea, the water-based or offshore cousin to a fire engine or fire truck, the fireboat is a vessel that has onboard water pump used to fight and extinguish fires both on other ships, as well as along the coastline, on docks and in ports and warehouses. Consider a situation where a ship in the ocean is caught on fire and you have been given the task to extinguish the fire.

Problem Statement

There is no problem statement for this event.

Judging Panel & Criteria

Judging criteria for round 1:
1.Number of lifeboats rescued- The team which will bring maximum lifeboats will get higher points(0 to 25 points).
2.Timing- If the teams complete the task in time they will get extra 10 points
3.Number of touches- For each touch with the wall the team will get -1 point

Judging criteria for round 2:
1.Number of candles extinguished- The team which will extinguish maximum number of candles will get maximum points(0-30 points)
2.Timing- If the teams complete the task in time they will get extra 10 points
4.Number of touches- For each touch with the wall the team will get -1 point

Rules and Regulations

1. Teams can consist of up to 4 members (maximum).

2. All the teams must follow the guidelines and time limit given.

3. Plagiarism is strongly discouraged, and any team found indulging in unfair practices will be disqualified from the event. If the violation is found to be serious, they can also be disqualified.

4. The decision of the judges is final and binding on all participants

Prizes & Rewards

All participating teams will receive certificates of participation from Samudramanthan, IIT Kharagpur. The top submissions may be published on our social media pages to appreciate their unique designs and design approach.

To The Rescue 2021 will award cash prizes to the top 3 participating teams on the basis of the presentation made in round 2. The overall prize money is ₹ 15,000/- for this event. The winners will also receive e-Certificates from Samudramanthan and a mention on our social media pages.
